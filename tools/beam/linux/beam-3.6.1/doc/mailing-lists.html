Mailing Lists

We provide two mailing-lists:

beam-announce
This is a read-only mailing list. New BEAM releases will be announced here (and only here).
To subscribe to beam-announce, click on this link and follow instructions.

beam-user
This low-volume mailing list is for BEAM users to post to. It is the most effective way to get our attention (as opposed to contacting one of us directly). Suggestions for new BEAM features or questions regarding our documentation should be posted here. Please do not post bug reports here. We have a specific page for that. Note, that you do not have to subscribe to this mailing list in order to post. You can simply send email to beam-user@edaweb.fishkill.ibm.com.
To subscribe to beam-user, click on this link and follow instructions.
